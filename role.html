Our Role: Fostering Awareness and Action

What Can We Do As a Community?

Addressing housing affordability is a complex challenge, but Rosmini College can play a vital role in raising awareness, fostering support, and encouraging positive change. While individual actions might seem small, our collective efforts can create a significant impact. We believe that by working together, we can contribute to a more equitable and stable housing future for everyone in our wider community.

  • Educate Ourselves & Others: Share reliable information about housing affordability with friends, family, and classmates. Understanding the real statistics and challenges helps in advocating for informed and effective solutions.
  • Support Local Initiatives: Identify and support local community groups, charities, and non-profit organizations in Auckland that are actively working on housing solutions. This can be through volunteering your time, making donations, or simply spreading awareness about their crucial work.
  • Advocate for Policy Change: Engage in respectful discussions about local and national housing policies with community leaders and elected representatives. Every voice contributes to the demand for a more just and accessible housing market.
  • Be a "Good Sort": Look out for and support families within our own community who may be experiencing housing insecurity. Sometimes, a kind word, a shared resource, or simply offering a listening ear can make a world of difference to someone facing hardship.
  • Promote Sustainable Practices: Consider supporting sustainable urban development and housing projects that aim to increase housing supply in an environmentally and socially responsible way.

By embracing these roles, fostering empathy, and staying continually informed, the Rosmini community can contribute meaningfully towards creating a future where access to safe, secure, and affordable housing is a reality for all.
